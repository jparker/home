From 27 Mar 2011 through 30 Oct 2011 the United Kingdom observes British Summer Time which is eight hours ahead of Pacific Daylight Time. When it is 8 o'clock in the morning in Los Angeles, it is 4 o'clock in the afternoon in the UK.
