Time Conversion

The UK and Ireland are 8 hours ahead of Pacific Time.

When it is 8:00AM in Los Angeles...
...it will be 4:00PM in the UK.
When it is 8:00AM in the UK...
...it will be 12:00AM in Los Angeles.

(Actually, Daylight Savings in the UK and Ireland begins on 29 March. As California enters Daylight Savings on 8 Mar, the UK and Ireland will only be seven hours ahead for the first several days of the trip.)
