Time Conversion

The UK and Ireland are 8* hours ahead of Pacific Time.

When it is 8:00am in Los Angeles...
...it will be 4:00pm the same day in the UK.
When it is 8:00pm in the Los Angeles...
...it will be 4:00am the next day in the UK.

* Actually, Daylight Savings in the UK and Ireland begins on 29 March, so for the first week they will be only seven hours ahead.
